LEAD IN EMPOWERMENT FOR ACCELERATED DEVELOPMENT (LEAD) is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming rural communities through innovative and sustainable solutions. We work to alleviate poverty, improve food security, provide access to education, and empower youth in agriculture.

VISION

To be the leading NGO in the provision of innovative sustainable technologies.

Promote ICT and access to health care and education to enhance rural development.

MISSION

To empower communities through the provision of sustainable innovative technologies,

access to health care and education for improved food security, alleviate poverty, and social injustice.

AIMS AND MAIN OBJECTIVES

To alleviate poverty, promote ICT, access to health care, education, and improve both food security and welfare.
